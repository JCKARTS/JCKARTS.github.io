About The Band

Founded in 2008 by Jiaro Jaramillo and Chris Lopez in Boyle Heights California. Agrezor is a thrashmetal project set to tear shit up. After over a decade of rocking, Luis Campos took over drums in 2018, and Joshua Kalmar took over Bass in 2023.
